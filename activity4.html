Centered Alignment


Text is aligned to the left edge of its container. This is the default alignment for most paragraphs in written documents and web pages. This Creates a clean, readable flow with consistent indentation at the beginning of each line.

Text is centered horizontally within its container. This Creates a visually balanced effect, often used for headings or short phrases.

Text is aligned to the right edge of its container. This can be less common than left alignment, but has specific uses.
